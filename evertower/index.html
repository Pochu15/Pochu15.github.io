Evertower Lite

Introduction

Evertower is an upcoming mobile game where you play as Cesium, a powerful mage with a very important task: To reach the top of the tower. Although he doesn't quite remember why...

Climbing this tower seems to be a simple task. However, it's not so simple, as this tower is crowded with enemies which will prevent you from climbing it. But why?

Clearly, something's wrong with this tower, as it seems to change it's structure very often.The only way to discover why this happens is to climb to the top, and try to recover your memory!

Discover the mysteries of this tower as you play through different runs!

The game

The basement

You'll start the game at the basement, located at the underground of the tower. There, you'll find the bookstand, which will allow you to unlock permanent upgrades or spells using Knowledge Points.

Bookstand

The bookstand will allow you to spend your Knowledge Points

As you get permanent upgrades using the bookstand, you'll become stronger, which means that you'll survive longer. Thus, you'll earn more KP, allowing you to get even more upgrades and continuing the cycle!

The basement will also offer different functionalities in the future. As you can see in the picture, there's an elevator, which is still under development ;)

Once you're done healing up at the basement, you'll be prepared to climb the tower using the stairs, which will start your run!

An evertower run

Nobody knows what you're going to find when you cross the stairs, as the tower is constantly changing it's structure! This means that every run will be different.

As for now, only enemies, chests and crafting tables will spawn. However, I'm already working on new stuff like shops, and I wouldn't mind developing other rooms.

boo ;)

Chests will give you items which will increase your stats, or potions so you can heal up. They can also give you gold and magic dust.

Enemies and battle system

Enemies will spawn across the dungeon. Once you get close to them, they'll start chasing you. If they get really close, you'll start battling. with up to three enemies at once

Battling works in turns. Enemies will use their spells on their turn (which means that they'll probably hit you). Each enemy type has their own spells, and every type is different!

Previous Next

When your turn comes, you'll draw a spell from your deck. Then, you can use a spell from your hand or use your action spell. Spells are consumed when used, so use them wisely, as you don't want to run out of spells while battling!

Most of the spells use mana. If you don't have enough mana, you won't be able to cast the spell.

Be careful! If you run out of health points while battling, you'll lose the run and you'll have to start from the basement again!

Winning a battle gives you loot. Most enemies will give you Gold, Knowledge Points and Magic dust.

Note: the game is at a very early state. Thus, many footage shown might actually change in the future. Stay tuned to see the game evolution!
